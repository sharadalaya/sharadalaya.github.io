What could be the purpose of life ... ... other than life itself ?
To stay alive and ensure that life continues.

Staying alive requires knowing truths.
Knowing which mushrooms are tasty and nutritious and which ones are poisonous.
Knowing how to surive a global viral pandemic.
Knowing how to turn an asteroid heading towards us into ashes.
Truth gives us godly powers, would we also need godly character.
But what is truth? Is there something like absolute truth?
If it helps us survive better - it's must be truth. A white lie must be truth?

Life is long and hard - a tiny bit of beauty would add a lot of joy.
All work and no play makes jack a dull boy.
But isn't the pursuit of truth beautiful all by itself ? To be lost in a quest.

Survival of just oneself or everyone ?
Which way would avoid the tragedy of the commons ?
With some trust and some co-operation within the largest possible collective ?
We don't want to end up in the Nash equilibrium of Prisoner's Dilemma.
