A belief that
  1. The purpose of life is life itself - to stay alive and ensure that life continues.
  2. Staying alive requires knowing truths.
  3. Life is long and hard - a bit of beauty would add a lot of joy.
Caveats:
[1] To avoid the Nash equilibrium of Prisoner's Dilemma; to avoid the tragedy of the commons, survival is through the trust and co-operation within the largest possible collective.
[2] The pursuit has no end, it is continuous and never ending; no matter how far we have already come.
[3] The pursuit of truth is beautiful all by itself. But beauty cannot stand alone without truth.
[4] Truth gives us godly powers but these powers must be wielded with equally godly character.

What's with the name?

Shaarada / Saraswathi / Benzaiten / Bhaarathi - is the oriental Goddess of knowledge, music, art, speech, wisdom, learning, rivers - all that is true and beautiful.
Aalaya - is sanskrit for abode.
