We religiously believe that

The purpose of our lives[1] is the obsessive and compulsive[2] pursuit[3] of Truth[4], Godliness[5] and Beauty[6].

What's your name again?

Shaarada / Saraswathi / Benzaiten / Bhaarathi - is the oriental Goddess of knowledge, music, art, speech, wisdom, learning - The Goddess of all that is true and beautiful in this world.
Aalaya - is sanskrit for abode.
Shaaradaalaya - The abode of the Goddess of truth and beauty.

Note that SHE is omnipresent in every being and every spec in which you can find truth and beauty. This is a very small humble honest (probably inconsequential) attempt to bring together ideas and people and discover some new beautiful truths, to together achieve godliness in our some of our abilities and godliness in some of our characters and to spread the joy of beauty by just one more bit.

This, is our form of worship, our idea of heaven, our purpose.

Tldr; References:

[1] Purpose of our lives - Life is about surviving, if we don't survive then we are not alive. It is not the survival of a single individual but us as a whole.
[2] Obsessive and Compulsive - An almost (but not entirely) irrational belief. Call it religious if you may. Because many a times rational decisions at individual level leads us all to the well known horrible Nash equilibrium of Prisoner's Dilemma. To the tragedy of the commons.
[3] Pursuit - We are always in pursuit because our goal is infinitely far away. No matter how far we have already come.
[4] Truth - The pursuit of truth is beautiful by itself. Side effects help us achieve godly abilities.
[5] Godliness - If we are to surive then we need some (if not all) godly abilities. Ability to snap a finger and stop the virus, to snap a finger and turn an asteroid to ashes. And with such godly powers comes the need for godly character that we need to build.
[6] Beauty - Life is long and hard, there is no point just surviving it, it'd be great if every moment was filled with joy.
